Nintendo Entertainment System

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_video_game_consoles

The Nintendo Entertainment System, or the NES, was released in July 15, 1983, and was one of Nintendo's first home video game to be released outside of Japan. The NES was one of the bestselling consoles at that time, selling 61.91 million units worldwide.
